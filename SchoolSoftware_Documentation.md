# Connect 2 Campus - Smart School Management System

## 1. Executive Summary
**Connect 2 Campus** is a comprehensive, cloud-based School Management System designed to digitize and automate the entire academic and administrative lifecycle of an educational institution. 

Bridging the gap between **School Admin, Teachers, Students, and Parents**, the software offers a seamless **Web Portal** and a dedicated **Android Mobile Application**. It replaces manual paperwork with smart, real-time digital workflows, ensuring efficiency, transparency, and enhanced communication.

## 5. Technology Stack (Why it's Robust)
Built on the latest modern web technologies ensuring speed, security, and scalability.
